Cheesecake Brownie Squares

These gooey brownies are a definite crowed pleaser

Ingredients:

  • 1 package fudge brownie mix (13-inch x 9-inch pan size)
  • 2 packages (3 ounces each) cream cheese, softened
  • 6 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 can (16 ounces) chocolate frosting

Directions:

  1. Prepare brownie mix batter according to package directions. Spread 2 cups into a greased 13x9-in. baking dish; set aside
  2. In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, butter, sugar, flour and vanilla until smooth
  3. Add eggs; beat on low speed just until combined. Spread evenly over brownie batter. Top with remaining brownie batter
  4. Cut through batter with a knife to swirl
  5. Bake at 350Â° for 28-32 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out with moist crumbs (brownies may appear moist)
  6. Cool completely on a wire rack. Spread frosting over brownies
